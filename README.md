# Service Quality Performance Analysis
## Project Overview
This project allows users to evaluate six key airline service metrics—including cabin staff service, entertainment, food, seat comfort, ground service, and value for money—across aircraft types, traveler segments, geographic regions, and time periods.
## Business Questions
1. How does customer satisfaction vary across aircraft types, customer segments, and service metrics?
2. Which customer segments and service areas should British Airways prioritize to maximize improvements in customer satisfaction?

## Key Insights
- Cabin staff service consistently outperformed entertainment and food & beverages, suggesting that onboard amenities present the greatest opportunity for improving overall customer satisfaction.
- Customer satisfaction differed across aircraft models, with Boeing 777-200 and A380 receiving higher cabin staff ratings than several other aircraft types.
  (Supported by: Average Cabin Staff Service by Aircraft)
- Entertainment received the lowest average customer rating (1.4/5), making it the weakest-performing service category and the highest-priority area for improvement.
- Food & Beverages also received relatively low customer ratings (2.4/5), indicating that onboard dining is another major driver of customer dissatisfaction.
- The A321, A320, and A319 consistently received the lowest entertainment ratings among all aircraft types, suggesting that these aircraft should be prioritized for in-flight entertainment upgrades.
  
## Business Recommendations
1. Prioritize entertainment upgrades on the A321, A320, and A319 fleets by improving in-flight entertainment systems and expanding available content, as these aircraft consistently received the lowest entertainment ratings.
2. Improve the onboard dining experience by enhancing food quality and menu variety, particularly on aircraft or customer segments where food ratings consistently underperform.
3. Maintain the high standard of cabin staff service while reallocating improvement efforts toward lower-rated service categories to maximize customer satisfaction within available operational resources.
